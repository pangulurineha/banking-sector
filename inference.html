INFERENCE FROM SURVEY DATA

Based on the survey responses from 50 individuals, the following key insights were inferred:

  • Total number of people who experienced banking frauds and online scams: 9
  • Total number of people who have not experienced banking frauds and online scams: 41
  • Total number of people aware of banking frauds and online scams: 35
  • Total number of people unaware of banking frauds and online scams: 15

These figures emphasize the need for broader awareness programs. While some customers are vigilant and informed, a significant number remain unaware of banking fraud risks, especially in rural areas. Educating this demographic is crucial to improving the overall security landscape.
